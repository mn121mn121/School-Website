About the School:

Tagore Academy is also known as Rabindra Bhavan. The school was established in 1995. Tagore Academy is a Co-Ed school affiliated to Council for Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE). It is managed by Tagore Society. It is located in Sakchi, Jamshedpur, East Singhbhum, Jharkand - 831001.

Tagore Academy started functioning under the governing body of the Tagore Society, an association that works for the promotion of Indian culture through education.
